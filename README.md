## üíª Introduction
A web game site designed with a **Squid Game** concept.

Play **Puzzle**, **Up-Down**, and **Muk-Jji-Ppa** games and record your ranking!

Easily access the site on mobile by adding it to your home screen!

### 5Ô∏è‚É£ Puzzle Game

<img src="https://github.com/matchlessNostril/SquidGame/assets/144131324/44ada8e1-268b-484e-a5b6-3056a6d94d2d" width="800" />

- Wait until the puzzle shuffles üòÖ
- On **Desktop/Laptop**, use the mouse or arrow keys to move pieces
- On **Mobile**, slide to move pieces
- Shows how many pieces are in the correct position
- Choose from **4 different puzzle designs** in the Custom Image section
- **Show Solution** toggle removes the correct image preview
- **Dim incorrect tiles** darkens misplaced pieces
- **Show numbers** displays the correct positions of pieces
  - **Invert number colors** reverses text colors
- Click **RE-SHUFFLE** to reshuffle the puzzle
- Once completed, the timer stops and the **game result screen** appears

### 6Ô∏è‚É£ Up-Down Game

<img src="https://github.com/matchlessNostril/SquidGame/assets/144131324/7a77113f-3c39-4241-8371-024a2f5ee783" width="800" />

- Press **Game Start** to begin
- Guess a number between **1 and 100** within **5 attempts** to win
- Use **Time Stop** to pause time or **Game Reset** to restart
- The **faster** and **fewer attempts** you use, the **higher the score**
- If you run out of attempts or guess correctly, the **game result screen** appears

### 7Ô∏è‚É£ Muk-Jji-Ppa Game

<img src="https://github.com/matchlessNostril/SquidGame/assets/144131324/6702b17f-576d-456d-8520-3a42d4b24ce2" width="800" />

- Click **Muk, Jji, or Ppa** to play
- Earn **10 points** for each win
- If you win, the next round starts **after 3 seconds**
- You have **5 lives**, losing removes **one life**
- Losing all lives moves you to the **game result screen**

### 8Ô∏è‚É£ Game Results (Rankings Available in Online Mode Only)

<img src="https://github.com/matchlessNostril/SquidGame/assets/144131324/a1fe4018-b461-463a-a029-626562ecaefe" width="800" />

- Displays your **game score**
- Shows **Top 10 rankings** for the game
- View your **highest ranking and best score**
- Click **Restart** to play again
- Click **Game List** to return to the game selection screen
